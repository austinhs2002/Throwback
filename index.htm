Yahoo Pager

Welcome to my 90s Internet Memorial! This page is dedicated to Yahoo Pager, one of the early instant messaging services that helped millions stay connected online in the late 1990s.

Yahoo Pager Features

Yahoo Pager allowed users to send real-time messages, create buddy lists, and even participate in group chats, long before social media platforms existed.

Legacy

Its simple interface and fun emoticons made online communication feel personal and lively, laying the foundation for modern chat apps.
