Welcome to my 90s Internet Memorial! This page is dedicated to Yahoo Pager, one of the early instant messaging services that helped millions stay connected online in the late 1990s.

Yahoo Pager allowed users to send real-time messages, create buddy lists, and even participate in group chats, long before social media platforms existed. Its simple interface and fun emoticons made online communication feel personal and lively, laying the foundation for modern chat apps.

Click Here to change the fun fact! Yahoo Pager supported group chats, which was rare for the late 1990s.
