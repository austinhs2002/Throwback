Angelfire

Angelfire was one of the earliest free web hosting services, allowing users to create personal web pages with text, images, and HTML. It became a popular destination for hobbyists, artists, and teenagers experimenting with web design in the late 1990s.

Learn more »

Customization

Users could customize their pages using templates or by editing HTML directly, adding guestbooks, animated GIFs, and even background music.

Legacy

Angelfire helped teach a generation how websites worked and how to build their own online presence.
